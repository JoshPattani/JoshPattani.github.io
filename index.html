About Me

Hey there!

I'm a carpenter from the City of Brotherly Love, turned welder when I became a Colorado transplant several years ago and now a student at the University of Colorado - Boulder. I've built my fair share of residential and commercial construction projects and traveled the country building rock climbing walls, expanding my skills in welding, masonry, rope access and equipment operation. Now, I'm pursuing a degree in Creative Technology and Design, exploring my passion for creativity, inclusivity, and technology. I love learning and experimenting with new tools and techniques in design, programming, fabrication, and digital content creation. When I'm not busy studying or working on projects, you can find me rock climbing, cultivating culinary mushrooms, or practicing my disc golf skills. I'm excited to see where my journey takes me and to share my experiences with others.

Thanks for stopping by!
