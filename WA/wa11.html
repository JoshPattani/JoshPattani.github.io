The Gang Gets a Quote Generator

This app will generate random quotes from the greatest sitcom ever made, It's Always Sunny in Philadelphia. Click the "Generate New Quote" button to generate a random quote from the show. Click the "Show Episode" button to reveal the origin season and episode of the quote (shown in mobile).


This quote generator was extended from the previous assignment by adding images of most characters being quoted. The style of the page is influenced by the show's aesthetics. Links to some available streaming options are added below.


Quotes are sourced from Sunny Quotes API.
